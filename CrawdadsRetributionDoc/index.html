Project Outline

The goal of this project was to create a game using Arduino electronics that can interact with a website using P5.js for graphics and Tone.js for sound. The arduino model that I used was the Arduino Uno in which I connected 3 LEDs, a button, and a joystick. The game itself is a simple 2D game where the player controlls a crayfish using the joystick to move around and pressing the button to attack. Variouse sprites and drawings are used to create the visuals of the game. Using Tone.js I was able to create a simple soundtrack with two seperate audio loops. One loop plays in both the Title/game over screen and another loop that plays during the gameplay. There are also royalty free sound effects that play during the player attack and whenever the Monster Fish appears.

Game Narative

In Crawdads Retribution you play as a crayfish whose home was just destroyed by a monster fish. You must enact vengeance on the monster fish, but you can’t do it on an empty stomach. Your goal is to eat smaller sea creatures such as worms and smaller fish to keep your strength up for the arriving battle. The monster fish will make various appearances on the right side of the screen and it is up to the player to use the crayfish’s pinching attack to damage him. The player is also tasked with keeping the hunger of the crayfish up or it will be game over.

Arduino Circuitry

The arduino side of the project consists of 3 LED (green, yellow, and red) designated for the hunger status of the player. The player will use both the button and the joystick to control the crayfish. the joystick will be used to move the crayfish while the button will be used for the pinching attack.

Future Development

In the future I would like to add various amounts of gameplay additions such as:

  • Crayfish abilities
    • Instead of just a pinching attack it would be cool to explore other abilities such as laser eyes, speed boost, and a biger claw attack.
  • More enemy types
    • Instead of just the three enemies consisting of worms and fish I would like to add more such as different insects and maybe some enemies that fight back.
  • Difficulty changes
    • I would also like to add different dificulty settings that the player can choose to give themselves a greater challenge. Theese dificulty settings may be used to increase boss health, damage, and potentially make enemyies spawn more or less.
  • Extra gameplay mechanics
    • I would also like to explore differen gameplay mechanics such as potentialy letting the monster fish attack the player forcing the player to either dodge or block. I would also like to add enemies that can attack the player as mentioned before.
